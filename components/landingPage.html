What is ROAS School of Marketing?

The ROAS School of Marketing isn't your typical online course platform.

It's a place built out of frustration with the surface-level education most marketers receive and out of a desire to fix that.

Founded by Syed Sohail (Roasguy), this school was born with one clear belief: Marketing isn't about learning dashboards. It's about understanding people.

Here, we don't just teach you how to press buttons on ad platforms. We dive deep into the why behind every scroll, click, and conversion.

So what makes us different?

We focus on psychology-backed strategies, not platform hacks.

We blend real campaign experience with storytelling and buyer behavior.

We simplify concepts so they're easy to learn, fun to apply, and impossible to forget.

We teach marketers how to use modern tools like AI to work faster and smarter.

We focus on practical execution so you can apply what you learn immediately.

We train you to think like a marketer, not just follow step-by-step tutorials.

The Mind Behind Roas School of Marketing

From humble beginnings to building a school that teaches what others don't.

A boy born in a small area of Hyderabad, Syed Sohail better known today as Roasguy always had bigger dreams. After completing his Bachelors in CSE and Masters in IT, it wasn't code or tech that pulled him in. It was marketing. Something about how people think, buy, and behave always made him curious. That curiosity turned into a full-time career. But in his early days, he found the same problem everywhere. Every course taught how to run ads, not why people respond to them. They explained buttons, not behavior. So he took a different route. He read books. Ran experiments. Failed. Improved. And slowly uncovered the deeper side of marketing most people skip. Over the last 6+ years, Sohail has worked on real strategies, real ads, and real business challenges developing a perspective that's rooted in buyer psychology, not just platform tricks.

That's where the idea hit him:
"What if I build a school that teaches marketing the way it should be taught?" Today, that idea is real. And so is his mission to become one of the top marketers in the world, and take ROAS School of Marketing across the globe.

And we're just getting started. This is more than a marketing school.  It's a MOVEMENT to raise the bar of marketing education in India and soon, the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What exactly will I learn in these courses?

You'll learn how marketing actually works. Not just how to run ads, but how to think like a marketer. We focus on buyer psychology, messaging, content, and strategy so every campaign you run connects and converts.

2. Are these courses only for people who already know marketing?

No. Our courses are built for beginners, business owners, freelancers, and even marketers who want to level up. Everything is taught in a clear, simple, and practical way.

3. How long will I have access to the course?

You'll get full access for 1 year from the date of purchase. You can watch and rewatch the content anytime during that period.

4. What if I don't like the course after enrolling?

We offer a refund based on the course duration and how much content you've accessed. If you're not satisfied, reach out to us and we'll sort it out.

5. Will more courses be added in the future?

Yes. ROAS School of Marketing is expanding. More courses on funnels, content, strategy, and brand building are on the way.

6. Can I ask questions or get help during the course?

Yes. You'll get access to our private community where you can ask questions, share feedback, and connect with others.
