Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 18, 2026

At Roas School of Marketing, we stand by the quality of our content and the learning experience we provide. However, we understand that sometimes a course may not meet your expectations.

We offer a flexible refund policy based on the following conditions:

1. Eligibility for Refund

You may request a refund if:

  • The request is made within the first 30 days of your course enrollment
  • You have not completed more than 30% of the course content
  • You provide a valid reason for dissatisfaction so we can improve our offerings

2. Partial Refunds (Case-by-Case)

If you have accessed more than 30% of the course, a partial refund may be granted depending on:

  • The amount of content consumed
  • The time elapsed since enrollment
  • Your feedback and reason for refund

All such cases will be reviewed manually by our team.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us with the following details:

  • Your full name
  • Course name
  • Date of purchase
